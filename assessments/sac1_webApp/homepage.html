Learning Mandolin is a great hobby!

Learning Resources

Learning Mandolin can be difficult at the start, but it quickly gets much easier to learn new songs, rhythms and strumming patterns. A great resource I use a lot is a site (and YouTube channel) called MandoLessons. It has an extensive library of Mandolin lessons, tabs and tutorials.

Another great site which is better for learning chords is MandoTabs. Its database of songs stretches on into the distance, and includes many popular songs, unlike MandoLessons.

I have compiled a list of Mandolin songs, along with the necessary resources to learn them. Most of the songs are for beginners,
since that's the level I'm at.



Useful Tools




Mandolin Cafe is one of the best resources there is when it comes to advice, tabs, techniques and much more. They even have a classifieds section where you can buy and sell used instruments, and they do giveaways every now and then of high quality mandolins.

History

The history of the Mandolin is long and interesting, and I would recommend reading up on it no matter what skill level you are.
