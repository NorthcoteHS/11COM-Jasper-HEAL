The Tab Index

This is a collection of my favourite tabs, and ones I found useful for learning. I've tried to include a range of songs, from country to pop, and everything in between. There are sound files available to be played, as well as links to instructional videos/tabs etc.

Eddie Vedder - Rise

Probably the first song I learnt. It is fun to play, and a good introduction to chords. Sadly there is a severe lack
of notation on it, but it shouldn't be too hard to learn.

The Road To Lisdoonvarna

A nice, simple tune that can be played slow or fast, depending on skill level. The tutorial I embedded is from
Mandolessons, a great site I wholeheartedly recommend.


Swallowtail Jig

One of my favourite songs to play. Like the previous tune, it can be played fast or slow at any skill level.


House of Gold

This song by 21 Pilots is played on the ukelele, and is fairly simple on the Mandolin. There is a tricky chord,
in the form of Am, but it is still relatively easy.
