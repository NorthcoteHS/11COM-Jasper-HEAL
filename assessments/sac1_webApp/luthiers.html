Luthier Directory

(only for australia)

Saga Instruments

A well known parent company based out of the United States, Saga Music has been producing high quality instruments for a long time.
They make Mandolins, Guitars, Banjos, Ukeleles, Violins and a few others. Some of their child companies are Kentucky, Rover and Blueridge.

Saga Music

Dunn Mandolins

Greg Dunn runs Dunn Mandolins out of his workshop in Brisbane, and offers a great selection of
instruments for reasonable prices, all handcrafted.

Dunn Mandolins

Gilchrist Mandolins and Guitars

Established in 1977 by Steven Gilchrist, all instruments are made in their workshop at Lake Gnotuk in
small batches so that all instruments are of the highest quality.

Gilchrist Mandolins

Duff Mandolins

Paul Duff built his first mandolin in 1982, and has been refining and perfecting his techniques ever since. All of his instruments are
made to order, and are built using traditional techniques that he says, guarantees quality.

Duff Mandolins
